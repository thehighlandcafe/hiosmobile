Unsure on how to use our digital Room Key? Take a look below.

  1. First, tap the credit_card icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Enter your booking Reference Number that you received in the receipt email from us.

  3. Click 'Next', and make sure NFC is switched on. If not, follow the on-screen instructions to turn it on.

  4. Wait for the card to load, then unlock your room within 10 seconds of it appearing on screen.
