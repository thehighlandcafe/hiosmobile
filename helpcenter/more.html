HiOSSocial Help

Unsure on how use HiOSSocial? Take a look below.

Joining HiOSSocial

  1. First, tap the more_vert icon on the top bar of the main HiOSMobile screen.

  2. Tap on the groups HiOSSocial option.

  3. Tap the button that says 'Accept Invite' - this will either direct you to the Discord app to accept the invitation.

  4. Once invited, you can use the service as you please.

Launching HiOSSocial

    Either:

  1. Launch Discord from your homescreen and navigate to the HiOSSocial page,

    2. or,


  2. Tap the more_vert icon on the top bar of the main HiOSMobile screen.

  3. Tap on the Launch HiOSSocial button at the bottom of the page.
