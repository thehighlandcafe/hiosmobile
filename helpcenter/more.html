Unsure on how get support from us? Take a look below.

Creating a Facebook Account

  1. First, go to facebook.com.
  2. Next, tap Create New Account and follow the steps to create and verify your Facebook account.

Messaging Us

  1. Tap the Customer Support option in the Help Center.
  2. You'll come to a page called Customer Support. If you don't have Messenger installed, install it from the button on the page. Otherwise, tap Contact Us.
