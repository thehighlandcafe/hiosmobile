Restaurant Help

Unsure on how to do certain things with this app for visiting The Highland Cafe™️? Take a look below.

Booking a Table

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Book a Table'.

  3. Fill in the form to book a table at The Highland Cafe™️.

Viewing our Menus*

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Menus'.

  3. Click on any menu on that page to download it to your device's 'Downloads' folder.

    4. *If you use an older Android device, ensure that the Storage permission is enabled.

Checking-In to Breakfast

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Check-In to Breakfast'.

  3. Fill in the form to check-in to breakfast.

Viewing our Locations

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Locations'.

  3. Zoom in on each location on the map, and look as you pleased.

Viewing our Offers

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Offers'.

  3. Scroll through all the current and expired offers. Blue = available, red = expired.
