Unsure on how to do certain things with this app for visiting The Highland Cafe™️? Take a look below.

Booking a Table

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Your HiCafe™️ Visit'.

  3. Tap the menu icon at the top right, and choose Booking.

  4. Fill in the form to book a table at The Highland Cafe™️.

Viewing our Menus

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Your HiCafe™️ Visit'.

  3. Tap the menu icon at the top right, and choose Menus.

  4. You can now scroll through our menus as you please.

Ordering Food

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Your HiCafe™️ Visit'.

  3. Tap the menu icon at the top right, and choose Order.

  4. Fill in the form to order food to your table at The Highland Cafe™️.

Checking-In to Breakfast

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the top bar.

  2. Tap 'Download Menus'.

  3. This will direct you to a Dropbox shared folder, containing all our menus. Tap any menu to view it.

Viewing our Locations

  1. First, tap the restaurant icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap 'Locations'.

  3. Zoom in on each location on the map, and look as you please.
